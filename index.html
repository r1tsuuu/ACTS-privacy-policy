Privacy Policy

AI Civic Triage System (ACTS)  ·  Built by Team CodeBlooded

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the AI Civic Triage System (ACTS) ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you interact with our application, including its integration with the Meta (Facebook) platform.

ACTS was built for the EXPE21ENCE YSES: The HackFest hackathon. Its purpose is to help local government units (LGUs) receive, classify, and act on civic reports submitted by citizens through a Facebook Page.

By using or interacting with our Facebook Page or this application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information through the Meta platform and our application:

From Facebook Page Webhooks:

  • Public posts made to, or on, the connected Facebook Page
  • The text content of those posts
  • Timestamps and public engagement metrics (e.g., reaction counts) associated with posts

From Facebook Login (if applicable):

  • Basic profile information such as name and profile picture
  • Email address (only if explicitly granted permission)
  • Facebook User ID

From Page Management APIs:

  • Page post content used for automated civic triage and classification
  • Comment data necessary for sending automated replies on behalf of the LGU Page

Automatically collected:

  • Log data such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited when accessing the dashboard
  • Session data for the purpose of maintaining the moderator dashboard session

We do not collect Messenger private messages, financial data, precise location data beyond what is extracted from public post text, or any sensitive personal categories of data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To receive and classify civic reports submitted through the Facebook Page
  • To extract location information and urgency signals to assist LGU moderators
  • To send automated acknowledgment replies to citizens via the Facebook Page
  • To display a moderation dashboard for authorized LGU staff
  • To improve the accuracy of our classification models over time using anonymized correction logs

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the civic triage functions described above.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With the LGU: Civic report content and classification results are shared with the authorized local government unit for which this system was deployed.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Service providers: We may use hosting providers (e.g., Railway, Render) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations.

All data processing occurs within the application and is not forwarded to any advertising network or data broker.

5. Data Retention

We retain civic report data (post text, classification results, and status history) for as long as necessary to fulfill the civic triage purpose or as required by applicable law.

  • Raw post text is retained in our database until manually deleted by an authorized administrator.
  • Correction logs and status history are retained for audit and model improvement purposes.
  • Session data is cleared upon logout or after a standard browser session expiry.
  • Log data is retained for up to 90 days.

As this application was built for a hackathon, all data may be permanently deleted after the event concludes unless the system is adopted for ongoing use.

6. User Data Deletion

In compliance with Meta Platform requirements, users have the right to request deletion of their data that we have collected via Facebook APIs.

To request deletion of your data:

  • Send an email to naguio.greg@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  • Include your Facebook username or the approximate date of your post so we can locate your record.
  • We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion via email.

You may also submit a data deletion request directly through Facebook: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Your Facebook Information → Off-Facebook Activity.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted HTTPS connections, environment-variable-based secret management, and access-controlled moderator dashboards.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our platform, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
